Our Services

Available exclusively for our members

Towing

Up to 4 service calls per membership year:

  • 1st tow: up to 20 miles
  • 2nd tow: up to 15 miles
  • 3rd tow: up to 10 miles
  • 4th tow: up to 5 miles

Tire Change

Flat tire? We’ll help replace it safely on the spot.

Fuel Delivery

Out of gas? We bring fuel to you. Fuel cost is separate.

Jump Start

Dead battery? We’ll help get your vehicle moving again.

Lockout

Locked out? We help you get back into your vehicle safely.

Coverage Rules

To ensure we can provide the best service, please note:

  • Members must live in the Portland Metro zone
  • Coverage is valid up to 50 miles from member residence
  • Extra miles are $6–$7 per mile
  • Vehicles must be newer than 1990

Membership Terms

Important information about your roadside coverage.

  • Any dispatch counts as 1 service call
  • Benefits activate 24–48 hours after signup
  • Services are for unexpected breakdowns only
  • Not valid for scheduled or repeated transport
  • Additional mileage billed separately
  • Coverage follows the member, not one vehicle
